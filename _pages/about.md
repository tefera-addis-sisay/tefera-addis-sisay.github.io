Tefera Addis SISAY is a Lecturer at [Bahir Dar University](https://www.bdu.edu.et/), Ethiopia. He holds an MEng in Information Systems Technology and Design (Cybersecurity) from the [Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD)](https://www.sutd.edu.sg), an MSc in Computer Science (Cybersecurity) from the [University of Trento](https://www.unitn.it/en), and a BSc in Software Engineering from [Bahir Dar University](https://www.bdu.edu.et/), Ethiopia.

His research focuses on cybersecurity and AI-driven communication systems, with emphasis on O-RAN security, anomaly detection, and performance degradation analysis in next-generation mobile networks using Non-RT RIC architectures. His work spans intelligent networked systems, secure cyber-physical infrastructures, and data-driven network automation.

## Research Interests

Cybersecurity, network security, wireless and O-RAN security, anomaly detection, and intrusion detection;  
Artificial intelligence, machine learning, and explainable/trustworthy AI for communication systems;  
Cyber-physical systems, IoT, distributed systems, and cloud-native network architectures.

## Research

My research develops secure, intelligent, and scalable methods for next-generation communication systems. I focus on improving reliability, automation, and robustness in O-RAN and software-defined networked infrastructures.

Key areas include:
- AI-driven network optimization and monitoring  
- Security analysis of wireless and cloud-native systems  
- Automated anomaly detection and performance diagnosis  
- Trustworthy and explainable AI for networked systems
