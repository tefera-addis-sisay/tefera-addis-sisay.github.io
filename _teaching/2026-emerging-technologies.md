## Course Description

**Emerging Technologies** introduces students to cutting-edge technologies transforming industries, society, and the future of computing. The course explores innovations in artificial intelligence, cloud computing, cybersecurity, Internet of Things (IoT), blockchain, 5G/6G communication systems, data science, virtual reality, and intelligent automation.

Students examine the technical foundations, practical applications, ethical implications, and societal impact of modern technological advancements through discussions, case studies, and research-based learning.

---

## Topics Covered

- Introduction to Emerging Technologies
- Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
- Internet of Things (IoT)
- Cloud and Edge Computing
- Blockchain and Digital Ledger Technologies
- Cybersecurity and Digital Trust
- Big Data and Data Analytics
- 5G/6G and Next-Generation Communication Systems
- Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR)
- Robotics and Intelligent Automation
- Smart Cities and Digital Transformation
- Ethical, Legal, and Social Implications of Technology

---

## Learning Outcomes

By the end of the course, students will be able to:

- Understand the principles behind major emerging technologies.
- Analyze real-world applications of modern technological innovations.
- Evaluate opportunities and challenges associated with digital transformation.
- Discuss ethical and societal impacts of emerging technologies.
- Explore future trends in computing, communication, and intelligent systems.
- Conduct research and present technology-based case studies.

---

## Teaching Methodology

The course includes:

- Interactive lectures
- Technology demonstrations
- Case-study discussions
- Research article analysis
- Student presentations
- Group projects and innovation activities

Students are encouraged to critically engage with future technologies and understand their impact on society and industry.
